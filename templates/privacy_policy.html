E-Tugal Privacy Policy 

1. Introduction 

Welcome to E-Tugal! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we 
collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application. 
By using E-Tugal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this 
policy. 

2. Information We Collect 

When using E-Tugal, we may collect the following types of personal information: 



 

Account Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, 
password, and other necessary information for registration. 



 

Profile Information: Information such as profile picture, bio, location, and user 
preferences. 



 

Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including tasks posted, 
tasks completed, reviews, and messages. 



 

Payment Information: If applicable, we collect financial information such as bank 
details or payment methods for processing payments within the app. 

3. How We Use Your Information 

E-Tugal uses the personal information we collect for the following purposes: 



 

To Provide and Improve Our Services: Your information helps us to operate the app, 
connect users, and improve the user experience. 



 

Task Matching: We use your location and profile information to connect you with 
relevant tasks or services in your area. 



 

Communication: We may use your contact details to communicate with you about your 
account, updates to the app, or customer support inquiries. 



 

Payment Processing: If applicable, your payment information will be used to process 
transactions between users. 



 

Security and Fraud Prevention: To ensure the security of our platform and protect 
against fraud or misuse. 

4. Sharing Your Information 

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following 
cases: 



 

With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties when you give 
us explicit consent to do so. 




 

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information if required by law or 
to protect the rights, property, or safety of E-Tugal or others. 



 

Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service 
providers who assist us in operating the app (e.g., payment processors, hosting services). 

5. Data Security 

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical 
and organizational measures to protect it. However, no system is completely secure, and we 
cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information. 

6. Your Data Rights 

You have the right to: 



 

Access and Update Information: You can view and update your personal information 
through your account settings. 



 

Delete Your Account: You can request the deletion of your account and personal data by

for legitimate business purposes.

Object to Processing: You may object to our use of your data for certain purposes, such

E-Tugal is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18 without parental consent. We
